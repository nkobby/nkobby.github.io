Research Interests

My research interests include neuro-symbolic AI, knowledge representation, automated reasoning, question answering using inferences drawn from knowledge graphs, distributed learning and reasoning systems, and explainable AI. I am currently working on hybrid (neuro-symbolic) and compositional AI architectures.

I ran the FRANK Lab where we discuss ongoing research related to the FRANK (Functional Reasoner for Acquiring New Knowledge) question-answering system and related work including meta- and object-level inference and dealing with inconsistencies in the reasoning process of large language models (LLMs)

I am also a co-founder at Cognate Systems, a software company in Ghana.
